Celonis, the global leader in Process Intelligence and Process Mining technology, seeks an experienced professional for this strategic role. Role Overview

The Client Value Partner (CVP) serves as the accountable lead for customer success with strategic accounts. This position involves engaging C-level executives to define long-term vision, mobilizing internal resources, and driving measurable business impact across various processes and industries.

Key Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain relationships with C-suite stakeholders
- Ensure proper resource staffing from cross-functional Celonis teams
- Lead the end-to-end customer value journey and resolve escalations
- Organize customer advisory boards and gather industry feedback
- Champion transformational initiatives and foster innovation
- Drive adoption targets and platform value realization

Required Qualifications

Candidates need "minimum 15 years of relevant customer success, strategy consulting, or related experience." Additional requirements include demonstrated expertise in large-scale transformation programs, change management practices, and the ability to navigate complex global organizations. Experience with AI-driven transformation is preferred.

Compensation & Benefits

Base salary plus bonus/commission, equity (RSUs), and comprehensive benefits including 24 weeks paid parental leave, unlimited PTO, hybrid work flexibility, wellness programs, and professional development opportunities.
