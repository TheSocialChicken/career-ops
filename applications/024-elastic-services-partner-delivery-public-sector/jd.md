**Title:** Regional Services Partner Delivery Manager - Public Sector
**Company:** Elastic
**Location:** Berlin, Germany
**Department:** Consulting - EMEA
**URL:** https://jobs.elastic.co/jobs?gh_jid=7635063

Elastic seeks an experienced Regional Services Partner Delivery Manager for Germany's Public Sector Services business. This position blends commercial strategy, customer relationships, and delivery execution, requiring the candidate to drive services bookings across federal, state, and regulated institutions while managing a complex engagement portfolio.

**Key Responsibilities:**
The role involves owning Public Sector Germany services bookings and revenue targets, executing territory strategies aligned with account plans, and maintaining accurate forecasting. Primary duties include leading services bids and negotiations, driving discovery for outcome-oriented offerings, and developing Statements of Work tied to measurable business outcomes. The manager will oversee concurrent engagements within cost and margin expectations, provide governance for strategic accounts, manage escalations, and act as a strategic advisor aligning services to customer mission objectives.

**Required Qualifications:**
- 8+ years in Professional Services, program delivery, or pre-sales within enterprise technology
- Demonstrated experience with German Public Sector organizations (federal ministries, state authorities, regulated entities)
- Proven commercial ownership of services bookings targets and complex contract negotiations
- Strong background delivering technology solutions in search, observability, analytics, or security
- Experience managing multiple concurrent engagements with governance responsibilities
- Fluency in German and English
- Willingness to travel up to 50% within Germany and occasionally internationally

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- Elasticsearch and related technologies familiarity
- PMP, PRINCE2, or Scrum Master certification
- Billable consultant or project manager experience
- Ability to obtain German security clearance if required
