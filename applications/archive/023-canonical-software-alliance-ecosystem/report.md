The Software Alliances team at Canonical collaborates with leading software companies to optimize their products with Ubuntu and Canonical software offerings. The team develops joint product roadmaps, drives delivery, and creates go-to-market strategies that enable differentiated offerings with robust product lifecycle support.

**The Role Entails:**
- Collaborating with product, engineering, alliance and marketing teams at partners to align on open-source strategies
- Capturing partner requirements, evaluating gaps, and identifying Canonical opportunities

- Creating partner and customer-facing materials including reference architectures, joint blueprints, webinars, presentations, videos, and white papers

- Influencing alliance partners to build with Canonical technology
- Up to 25% travel, including internationally, for internal conferences and partnership development

- Exceptional academic track record from high school and university
- Bachelor's degree in science, technology, and/or engineering

- Ability to work at both strategic and tactical levels
- Self-motivation for remote-first workplace environments

- Open source software and business models experience
