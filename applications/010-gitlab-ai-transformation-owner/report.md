## Job Description (archived)

**Title:** AI Transformation Owner, Marketing
**Location:** Remote — Canada, Ireland, United Kingdom, US
**Salary Range (US):** $152,800 – $259,200 USD
**Company:** GitLab

**Role Overview:**
GitLab seeks an AI Transformation Owner for its Marketing function. This hybrid product management and strategic role focuses on identifying where artificial intelligence can fundamentally reshape organizational operations. The position combines business strategy, hands-on agent building, and change management to drive measurable outcomes across the marketing team.

**Key Responsibilities:**

Strategy & Prioritization:
- Own the function's AI strategy aligned with executive priorities and business metrics
- Map end-to-end workflows to identify high-impact opportunities where "AI could let your org execute orders of magnitude faster"
- Manage intake of AI requests and ideas across the function
- Make strategic prioritization decisions focused on business outcomes

Adoption & Change Management:
- Drive adoption through channels, rituals, and feedback loops that make AI visible within teams
- Build and sustain a Champion network across sub-teams (5-10% time commitment)
- Coordinate with Enterprise AI to share patterns and learnings across the organization
- Challenge teams to reimagine workflows rather than simply automating existing ones

Hands-On Building & Operations:
- Build AI agents using no-code and low-code platforms
- Author and iterate on skill files that define agent behavior
- Configure MCP servers and tools to connect agents with business systems
- Own performance tracking and KPI evaluation for your function's agent fleet
- Remain adaptable as AI tools evolve rapidly

**Required Qualifications:**

Business & Strategic Skills:
- Deep operational knowledge of your function's processes and constraints
- Product management mindset with focus on intake, backlog, iteration, and adoption
- Strong communication and influence abilities
- Experience building peer networks or communities of practice
- Cross-functional understanding of how processes interconnect

Technical & Hands-On Skills:
- Ability to build working agents and configure skills without writing production code
- Fast learner ready to master no-code/low-code AI platforms and prompt engineering
- Strong conceptual understanding of AI capabilities (summarization, classification, generation, automation, agentic workflows)
- Ability to map structured and unstructured data flows
- Commitment to staying current with rapidly evolving AI landscape

**Team Structure:**
The role partners closely with Enterprise AI within the Enterprise Technology & AI organization. The AI Engineer provides technical delivery, platforms, and architecture; the Transformation Owner brings business context, no-code building, and adoption strategy.

**Benefits & Support:**
GitLab offers flexible paid time off, equity compensation, growth and development funds, parental leave, team member resource groups, and comprehensive health and financial benefits.

GitLab is an equal opportunity employer committed to building diverse teams. The company welcomes applications from candidates at varying experience levels and encourages underrepresented groups to apply.
