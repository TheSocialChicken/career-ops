Celonis, the global leader in Process Intelligence, seeks an experienced Client Value Partner to drive transformation with strategic customers. This role serves as the accountable lead for customer success, engaging C-level executives to define long-term vision for Celonis adoption.

The position requires leading cross-functional teams and managing executive relationships. You'll develop and maintain strong relationships with key stakeholders, including C-level internal/external executives while ensuring proper resource allocation across Celonis departments.

Value delivery involves owning the end-to-end customer value journey and driving adoption metrics. You'll resolve escalations and align Celonis initiatives with customer strategic objectives, establishing programme roadmaps and value plans collaboratively.

Essential skills include project management across multiple streams, ability to translate use cases for business impact, and comfort operating within ambiguous, fast-paced environments.
