**Head of Industry - Retail & CPG**
Location: Munich, Germany
Department: Value Engineering

Celonis is the global leader in Process Intelligence and the pioneer of Process Mining technology. As one of the world's fastest-growing enterprise SaaS companies, the organization invests heavily in advanced AI capabilities to turn data insights into immediate business action.

**The Team:**
The Celonis Retail & CPG Industry Practice operates with over 100 professionals across global accounts. This team combines go-to-market strategy, product development, and engineering to help clients achieve value through the Celonis software platform.

**The Role:**
In this position, you will lead the vision, strategy, and execution of the Retail & CPG Industry Practice. You'll work across go-to-market, product, AI, and engineering teams to build scalable software solutions. You'll serve as the internal and external representative for this industry vertical and be responsible for the success of clients, employees, and partners.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Establish industry practice vision and translate it into actionable strategy
- Build and lead cross-functional teams across value engineering, sales, and AI
- Partner directly with customers on solutions aligned to their business objectives
- Collaborate with product and engineering leaders on future development
- Identify and package scalable use cases across the platform
- Enable internal teams and partners in solution deployment

**Required Qualifications:**
- Demonstrated experience building and leading cross-functional teams solving enterprise Retail & CPG challenges
- Entrepreneurial background with 0-to-1 building experience
- Strong public speaking and presentation abilities
- Portfolio of successful enterprise software solutions
- Excellent communication skills translating complex concepts for diverse audiences

**What Celonis Offers:**
Employees receive equity through Restricted Stock Units, 24 weeks of paid parental leave for primary carers, unlimited PTO in applicable regions, flexible hybrid work arrangements, learning and development support, wellness benefits, volunteer opportunities, and inclusive workplace initiatives.
