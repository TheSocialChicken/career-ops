**Field CTO - AI & Enterprise Modernization**
Location: Munich, Germany / London, United Kingdom
Department: Value Engineering

Celonis is the global leader in Process Intelligence and the pioneer of Process Mining technology. As one of the world's fastest-growing enterprise SaaS companies, the organization invests heavily in advanced AI capabilities to turn data insights into immediate business action.

**The Role:**
The company seeks a visionary Field CTO with a "learn-it-all" mindset to serve as a high-impact technical advisor for strategic prospects and customers. The position involves acting as a peer to client executives, translating complex business transformations into scalable technical strategies while championing Process Intelligence, Agentic AI, and Enterprise Modernization.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Serve as trusted advisor to C-level executives, aligning strategic goals with Celonis technical vision
- Lead data, cloud, and innovation strategy for major global accounts using Capability Mapping
- Architect first-of-a-kind solutions using emergent technologies including Agentic AI
- Partner with Sales and Value Engineering to drive revenue through customer acquisition and strategic upselling
- Act as feedback loop between Product & Engineering teams and market
- Develop customer-facing position papers and presentations establishing thought leadership

**Required Qualifications:**
- 15-20 years relevant experience as CTO/CIO or Field CTO at major software vendor
- Proven ability presenting complex technical visions to C-suite stakeholders
- Deep expertise in Enterprise Modernization, EA Methodologies, and Agentic AI
- Strong background in Data Architecture, API ecosystems, and cloud-native SaaS solutions
- MSc in Computer Science or equivalent IT-related field
- Fluency in English; German, Spanish, French, or Italian proficiency advantageous

**Compensation & Benefits:**
Celonis offers Restricted Stock Units, 24 weeks fully paid parental leave, unlimited PTO, flexible hybrid work, learning programs, wellness initiatives, and annual Impact Days for volunteering.
