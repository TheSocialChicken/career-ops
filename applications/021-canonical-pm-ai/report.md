**Title:** Product Manager - AI
**Company:** Canonical
**Location:** Home Based - EMEA
**Department:** Product
**URL:** https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/canonical/jobs/6643476

Canonical seeks a Product Manager - AI to lead product strategy for innovative open-source solutions. The company is a leading provider of open source software and operating systems serving enterprise and technology markets, with Ubuntu as its flagship platform.

**About the Role:**
The position involves leading the product development lifecycle, collaborating across engineering, design, marketing, and sales teams. Responsibilities include conducting market research, analyzing industry trends, gathering customer feedback, and communicating product vision to stakeholders. The role requires overseeing product lines, facilitating team collaboration, and contributing to sales success through customer engagement.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Lead product development priorities and goals
- Address project challenges and align team priorities
- Conduct market research and identify opportunities
- Develop go-to-market strategies with marketing and sales
- Oversee product consistency across teams
- Facilitate cross-departmental collaboration

**Required Qualifications:**
- Strong academic background
- Understanding of Linux and open source communities
- Knowledge of computer architecture, application development, datacenters, or cloud
- Excellent English communication skills
- Problem-solving and innovation capabilities
- Strong work ethic aligned with open source values

**Desired Experience:**
- Product management experience in software industry
- Familiarity with cloud-native technologies, embedded devices, or networking
- Leadership experience with distributed teams
- Willingness to travel internationally up to 4 times yearly

**Compensation & Benefits:**
Distributed work environment, $2,000 annual learning budget, performance-based bonuses, comprehensive leave policies, and travel opportunities.
