The difference between someone with 8 years and someone with 15 is the difference between a person who needs to figure out the answers and a person who has already made the mistakes. The instinct is to frame the education background as a gap to overcome. Christiaan has spent his career in environments where adoption is the hard part. Schools are full of people who did not choose to change and who have strong institutional reasons to resist. If he can get a university faculty to adopt AI tools in eight weeks, he can get four agency delivery teams to use them in four.

This is not a steady-state Head of AI role. This is a founding role for RPW's AI-first transformation. The person who takes this in 2026 will define the culture, the tools, the SOPs, and the standards that govern how AI is used at the agency for the next several years. Christiaan has done exactly this at Windesheim (built the AI lab culture from scratch), at Aeres (redesigned the educational model school-wide), and at Spark! Living Lab (built a functioning innovation lab from zero). He knows what it takes to build a new system inside an existing organization without breaking what already works.
