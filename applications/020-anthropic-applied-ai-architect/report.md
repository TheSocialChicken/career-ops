**Applied AI Architect, Industries**
Company: Anthropic
Location: Munich, Germany
Department: Applied AI

**About Anthropic:**
Anthropic's mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. The team comprises researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working to build beneficial AI systems.

**About the Role:**
As an Applied AI team member, you'll serve as a Pre-Sales architect and trusted technical advisor helping large enterprises understand Claude's value and integration strategies. You'll combine technical expertise with customer-facing skills to architect LLM solutions addressing complex business challenges while maintaining safety and reliability standards.

Working with Sales, Product, and Engineering teams, you'll guide customers from discovery through deployment, helping them understand Claude's capabilities, develop evaluations, and design scalable architectures.

**Responsibilities:**
- Partner with account executives to translate customer requirements into technical solutions
- Serve as primary technical advisor throughout Claude adoption journeys
- Support customers building with Claude API and Claude for Work
- Create and deliver compelling technical content for diverse audiences
- Guide technical architecture decisions and integration into existing systems
- Help customers develop evaluation frameworks for measuring Claude's performance
- Identify common integration patterns and contribute insights to Product and Engineering teams
- Travel occasionally to customer sites for workshops and relationship building
- Maintain strong knowledge of latest LLM developments and implementation patterns

**You May Be a Good Fit If You Have:**
- 5+ years in technical customer-facing roles such as Solutions Architect, Sales Engineer, or Technical Account Manager
- Native German speaker with fluent English proficiency
- Enterprise customer experience navigating complex buying cycles
- Ability to communicate technical concepts to diverse stakeholders including executives and engineering teams
- Strong technical communication skills translating between business and technical stakeholders
- Experience designing scalable cloud architectures and enterprise system integration
- Comfort with Python
- Familiarity with LLM frameworks, tools, machine learning, or data science background
- Passion for cross-organizational collaboration and balancing competing priorities
- Teaching and mentoring skills
- Interest in safe and beneficial AI advancement

**Compensation & Logistics:**
Annual Salary: €190,000–€215,000 EUR
Minimum Education: Bachelor's degree or equivalent combination of education, training, and experience
Hybrid Policy: Expectation of at least 25% office time; some roles may require more
Visa Sponsorship: Available with reasonable effort
Application Deadline: Rolling basis; no specific deadline

**Additional Information:**
Anthropic encourages applications even if you don't meet every qualification. Representation matters in AI development. Anthropic only contacts candidates via @anthropic.com email addresses and never requests money or banking information before employment begins.
