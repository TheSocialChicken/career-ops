**Title:** Retail & Consumer Goods EMEA Leader
**Requisition ID:** SLSQ127R516
**Location:** Germany; London, United Kingdom; Netherlands; Paris, France
**Department:** Go To Market
**Office:** Remote - United Kingdom
**Company:** Databricks

**Overview**

This role involves architecting and executing a market-defining strategy for Retail and Consumer Goods in EMEA. The position requires building scalable go-to-market operations from the ground up, operating with "the mindset of a founder of a hyper-growth enterprise."

**Key Responsibilities**

- Designing and executing go-to-market strategies that establish C-level relationships and accelerate revenue growth across retail, consumer goods, restaurants, and wholesale sectors
- Identifying high-value use cases and converting them into systematic, scalable revenue programs
- Building partner ecosystems with ISVs, SIs, and alliances from first principles
- Creating sales plays tailored to EMEA's diverse geographies while supporting other regions
- Establishing Databricks as an authoritative voice through thought leadership, white papers, and industry event participation
- Serving as a trusted advisor across product, sales, pre-sales, marketing, and partner teams
- Developing industry-specific collateral and demand generation campaigns
- Building enablement content that elevates the entire verticalized sales organization

**Required Qualifications**

- Senior leadership experience in Retail or Consumer Goods
- Proven ability to operate in ambiguous environments with incomplete information
- Extensive go-to-market architecture experience on technology platforms
- Executive relationship-building capabilities with C-suite leaders
- Large-scale change initiative leadership experience
- Deep familiarity with data and AI landscapes and retail technology standards
- Understanding of European regulatory environments (GDPR, sustainability reporting, etc.)
- Complex vertical initiative management experience
- Sales team training and enablement proven success
- Fluency in English with strong proficiency in French or German

**About Databricks:** A data and AI company serving over 10,000 organizations worldwide, including more than 50% of the Fortune 500.
