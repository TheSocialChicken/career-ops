**Title:** AI Deployment Strategist
**Location:** Amsterdam, Netherlands
**Team:** Solutions
**Employment Type:** Full-time, Onsite
**Country:** Netherlands

**Role Overview:**
This position bridges business strategy and technical execution. The successful candidate will drive the adoption and deployment of Mistral's AI solutions by collaborating with enterprise clients from initial planning through production implementation.

**Key Responsibilities:**

Strategic Planning:
- Facilitate executive workshops identifying AI opportunities for business transformation
- Build AI adoption strategies with customers, showing feasible pathways to measurable value
- Partner with sales teams on ROI calculations and business case development

Solution Implementation:
- Design comprehensive AI systems integrating Mistral's technology into client operations
- Collaborate with Applied AI specialists on prototype development and production deployment
- Lead pilot initiatives and proof-of-concept projects

Client Success:
- Function as strategic advisor ensuring maximum value realization from Mistral investments
- Track business-focused performance metrics and report progress to sponsors
- Identify growth potential within existing client relationships

Cross-Team Collaboration:
- Bridge customer needs with internal Mistral teams
- Create reusable deployment frameworks and best practices
- Travel 30-60% annually for client engagement

**Required Qualifications:**
- Minimum 2+ years in client-facing technical and strategic roles
- Degree in relevant scientific discipline
- AI/ML/Data Science foundational knowledge
- Hands-on experience building and deploying AI applications using Python
- Strong executive communication abilities
- Business problem-solving expertise
- Sales frameworks knowledge (MEDDPICC) preferred

**Benefits Package:**
- 25 PTO days annually
- €60 monthly transportation allowance
- €170 monthly food allowance
- €60 sport/gym allowance

**Hiring Process:**
Candidates proceed through: screening call, manager interview (30 min), business acumen assessment (45 min), LLM technical evaluation (60 min), panel presentation with preparation, value discussion (30 min), and reference verification.
