**Title:** Software Alliance Ecosystem Manager
**Company:** Canonical
**Location:** Home Based - Americas; Home based - EMEA
**Department:** Alliances
**URL:** https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/canonical/jobs/6397940

The Software Alliances team at Canonical collaborates with leading software companies to optimize their products with Ubuntu and Canonical software offerings. The team develops joint product roadmaps, drives delivery, and creates go-to-market strategies that enable differentiated offerings with robust product lifecycle support.

**The Role Entails:**
- Collaborating with product, engineering, alliance and marketing teams at partners to align on open-source strategies
- Capturing partner requirements, evaluating gaps, and identifying Canonical opportunities
- Coordinating with marketing and product teams on messaging, content creation, and launch plans
- Creating partner and customer-facing materials including reference architectures, joint blueprints, webinars, presentations, videos, and white papers
- Driving Ubuntu adoption within targeted alliance accounts and working with partners on enablement training
- Influencing alliance partners to build with Canonical technology
- Up to 25% travel, including internationally, for internal conferences and partnership development

**What We're Looking For:**
- Exceptional academic track record from high school and university
- Bachelor's degree in science, technology, and/or engineering
- Experience in software development and system architecture
- Customer-facing experience in software engineering, product management, business development, or technology strategy
- Excellent communication skills across all stakeholder levels
- Ability to work at both strategic and tactical levels
- Self-motivation for remote-first workplace environments

**Nice-to-Have Skills:**
- Open source software and business models experience
- Team leadership, product management, or program management experience
- Marketing and go-to-market strategy understanding
- Sector experience in finance, telecommunications, automotive, or public sectors
- Knowledge of ISV and SaaS go-to-market approaches
- Post-graduate degree in science, technology, or engineering

**What Canonical Offers:**
- Distributed work environment with twice-yearly in-person team sprints
- USD 2,000 annual personal learning and development budget
- Annual compensation review and performance-driven bonuses
- Recognition rewards, holiday leave, maternity/paternity leave
- Employee Assistance Programme
- Travel opportunities and Priority Pass benefits for company events
