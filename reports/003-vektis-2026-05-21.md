**Stage and type:** Established Dutch non-profit / semi-public organization. Vektis collects and manages healthcare market data for the Dutch health insurance system. It is a stable, mission-driven organization with a public-service orientation — not a startup, not a commercial corporation. The culture is likely process-oriented, collaborative, and consensus-driven, with a focus on data quality and governance.

**Culture signals:** The JD language emphasizes coaching, facilitation, and psychological safety — Vektis is not asking for a change agent or a builder; it is asking for a steady facilitator of existing agile teams. The named recruiter (Maurice) and transparent salary suggest a human, approachable HR process. The healthcare data context implies regulatory care and measured decision-making.

**Physical/work environment:** Hybrid at Zeist office. Zeist is a quiet suburban town 30 minutes from Baarn — a comfortable commute. The office environment is likely conventional and professional, not a lab or maker space.

**Match against 2-year vision:** The 2-year vision is a hackerspace: small group, building new things, high trust, new territory. Vektis scores low on "new territory" and "building" — the organization is established and the role is to maintain agile cadences, not create them. The "high trust, small group" dimension is possible in a mid-size non-profit. Alignment is conditional: if the teams are genuinely early in their agile journey, there is a facilitation challenge worth engaging with; if they are mature teams needing ceremony management, it is a maintenance role.
