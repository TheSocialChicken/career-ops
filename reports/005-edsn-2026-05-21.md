## H) Culture, Personality & Long-term Fit

### H1 — Org Culture Assessment

**Stage and type:** Established Dutch public-utility-adjacent organization. EDSN (Energie Data Services Nederland) manages critical energy data infrastructure for the Dutch energy market — a semi-public, regulated context. The culture is process-driven, governance-focused, and stability-oriented. It is building SAFe structures, which signals a large-program, compliance-first organizational mindset.

**Culture signals:** The JD language emphasizes SAFe implementation, servant leadership, and ART facilitation. The requirement for PSM II/III before applying signals that credentials matter — this is a credential-valuing culture. The named contact and transparent salary are positive procedural signals, but the SAFe context implies heavy ceremony and framework compliance rather than innovation.

**Physical/work environment:** Hybrid at Amersfoort. Amersfoort is 15 minutes from Baarn — the best commute in this batch. The physical setting is a corporate/utility office.

**Match against 2-year vision:** The 2-year vision is a hackerspace: small group, building new things, high trust, new territory. EDSN scores poorly on almost every dimension of this vision. SAFe is arguably the most ceremony-heavy agile framework in existence — the opposite of a hackerspace. The energy data infrastructure domain is stable and regulated. The "new territory" dimension is absent. Alignment is very low.
