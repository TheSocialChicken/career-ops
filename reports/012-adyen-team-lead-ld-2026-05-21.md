Team Lead - Learning & Development

The L&D Core team drives Adyen's global learning strategy through interventions, communities of practice, and advanced tooling. As Team Lead, you architect this ecosystem by leading Subject Matter Experts and defining the strategic direction for learning at scale. Your work translates into "simple, unique, and bold" solutions aligned with company strategy.

What you'll do

- Define the North Star for 2026 strategy focused on strengthening leadership effectiveness and articulate long-term learning vision
- Build high-trust relationships across departments and communicate strategic rationale to stakeholders
- Position L&D Core as a primary organisational enablement driver integrated into key business groups
- Advocate for frictionless, learner-centred experiences that meet employees where they are
- Prioritise needle-moving projects solving real business problems
- Lead and coach team members to reach full potential while maintaining high performance standards
- Remove organisational friction to enable team delivery

Who you are

Required Experience

- Minimum 3 years managing teams of specialists in fast-paced tech or high-growth environments
- Deep expertise developing L&D programs within broader HR functions
- Ability to influence senior leadership across complex, global matrix organisations
- Proven track record translating business requirements into world-class learning strategies
- Comfort with modern learning technologies and demonstrating learning-business strategy connections
