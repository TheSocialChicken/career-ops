**Title:** Client Value Partner (CVP) - AI-Driven Transformation & Process Intelligence
**Location:** Zurich, Switzerland
**Department:** Value Engineering
**Company:** Celonis
**Job ID:** 7669258003
**Requisition ID:** 614x
**Recruiter:** Daniel Toth (d.toth@celonis.de)

Celonis, the global leader in Process Intelligence, seeks an experienced Client Value Partner to drive transformation with strategic customers. This role serves as the accountable lead for customer success, engaging C-level executives to define long-term vision for Celonis adoption.

Key Responsibilities

The position requires leading cross-functional teams and managing executive relationships. You'll develop and maintain strong relationships with key stakeholders, including C-level internal/external executives while ensuring proper resource allocation across Celonis departments.

Value delivery involves owning the end-to-end customer value journey and driving adoption metrics. You'll resolve escalations and align Celonis initiatives with customer strategic objectives, establishing programme roadmaps and value plans collaboratively.

Community building includes organising customer advisory boards and acting as the voice of the customer internally, fostering innovation and thought leadership opportunities.

Required Qualifications

Minimum 15 years in customer success, strategy consulting, or related transformation experience. Candidates need demonstrated expertise in large-scale transformation programs, change management, and executive engagement. Experience with AI-based transformation is valued.

Essential skills include project management across multiple streams, ability to translate use cases for business impact, and comfort operating within ambiguous, fast-paced environments.

Important Restriction: "We are currently only considering candidates who are already based in Switzerland and have the legal right to work in Switzerland. We are not able to offer relocation or visa sponsorship for this role."

Compensation: Base salary plus bonus/commission, equity, and comprehensive benefits including 24 weeks paid parental leave and unlimited PTO.
