This role leads the Forward Deployed Engineering motion across a business unit or region. The position combines technical expertise with sales alignment, serving as a trusted advisor to executives and strategic partner to account teams.

**Key Responsibilities:**
The successful candidate will "lead the Forward Deployed Engineering motion in your region, aligning closely with Sales, Delivery, and Field Engineering leadership." The role emphasizes mentoring junior managers, collaborating with partners like GSIs and cloud providers, and demonstrating technical depth in data and AI platforms. The position involves approximately 20% customer travel.

**Required Qualifications:**
Candidates should possess strong customer-facing experience in consulting and solutioning roles, with the ability to "engage in architecture-level discussions and translate technical designs into business outcomes." Deep industry expertise, professional services leadership background, and solid knowledge of data platforms are essential.

The ideal candidate combines excellent communication and negotiation skills with program management capabilities and experience in the Data & AI domain.

**About Databricks:**
Databricks serves as "the data and AI company," with over 10,000 organizations relying on its Data Intelligence Platform, including more than half the Fortune 500.
