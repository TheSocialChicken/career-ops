**If downlevelled to Senior TPM:** Accept if comp is at target range (€65k+) and there is a stated path to Staff with clear criteria within 12-18 months.

**Title:** Staff Technical Program Manager
**Location:** Remote — Germany, Ireland, Netherlands, United Kingdom
**Company:** GitLab
**Department:** Office of the CTO
**Job ID:** 8506618002

**Overview:**
GitLab seeks a Staff Technical Program Manager to direct significant cross-functional portfolios across engineering. This role manages complex, multi-team initiatives where scope is broad, stakeholders numerous, and direction unclear. Success requires creating clarity, enabling coordination, and delivering meaningful outcomes across multiple quarters.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Lead planning, execution, and delivery of high-priority, cross-functional programs
- Operate at portfolio level identifying dependencies, tracking progress, and managing risks
- Partner with Engineering Directors, VPs, Product leadership, and technical experts on priorities and execution plans
- Build structure in ambiguous spaces through frameworks, decision points, and communication cadences
- Translate technical architecture and infrastructure considerations into executable program plans
- Drive stakeholder coordination across Monetization, Fulfillment, Utilization, Legal, and engineering teams
- Communicate program status and risks to senior stakeholders with actionable recommendations
- Improve planning practices and support outcome-oriented execution at scale

**Required Qualifications:**
- Proven Staff or Senior Staff-level technical program management experience
- Success leading large, complex, cross-functional programs in distributed engineering environments
- Strong technical fluency with CI/CD, cloud infrastructure, APIs, and distributed systems
- Exceptional stakeholder management and executive communication skills
- Proficiency with GitLab, Jira, or comparable project management tools
- Ability to identify dependencies from technical discussions and create practical plans
- Demonstrated ownership, sound judgment, and collaboration in ambiguous environments

**Team Culture:**
The Technical Program Management team operates in an all-remote, async-first environment emphasizing direct communication, trust, and shared ownership. Work is high-visibility, high-impact, and plays a critical role in GitLab's scaling strategy.

**Benefits:**
GitLab offers health and wellness benefits, flexible paid time off, equity compensation, growth and development funds, parental leave, and team member resource groups.
