## Job Description (archived)

**Title:** AI Deployment Strategist
**Location:** Munich, Germany
**Team:** Solutions
**Commitment:** Full-time
**Workplace Type:** On-site

**Role Summary:**
This position bridges business strategy and technical execution. The successful candidate will drive the adoption and deployment of Mistral's AI solutions, working closely with customers from strategic vision to production implementation.

**Key Responsibilities:**

Strategic Discovery & Vision Setting:
- Facilitate executive workshops identifying business challenges where AI creates meaningful improvements
- Co-create AI adoption roadmaps with customers
- Develop business cases and quantify ROI alongside Account Executives

AI Solution Design & Deployment:
- Design end-to-end AI solutions integrating Mistral's models into customer infrastructure
- Partner with Applied AI teams on prototyping and production deployment
- Execute pilot projects and proofs-of-value demonstrations

Value Realization & Customer Success:
- Serve as strategic advisor guiding customers' AI initiatives
- Monitor KPIs tied to business outcomes
- Identify expansion opportunities within accounts

Cross-Functional Collaboration:
- Bridge customer needs with internal teams
- Develop reusable playbooks and best practices
- Travel 30-60% for on-site client engagement

**Requirements:**
- 2+ years in client-facing strategic/technical roles (consulting, value engineering, technical sales)
- Degree in relevant scientific field (Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering)
- AI/ML/Data Science foundational knowledge
- Hands-on Python experience building and deploying AI applications
- Strong business acumen and executive communication skills
- MEDDPICC or value-based selling experience preferred

**Benefits:**
- Competitive salary and equity
- Gym membership allowance
- Meal allowance
- Public transportation allowance
- Generous parental leave
- Visa sponsorship

**Hiring Process:**
1. Talent Acquisition screening call
2. 30-minute Hiring Manager Interview
3. 45-minute Business Acumen Interview
4. 60-minute Technical Deep Dive on LLMs
5. Panel (Home Prep & Restitution)
6. 30-minute Value Talk
7. Reference check
