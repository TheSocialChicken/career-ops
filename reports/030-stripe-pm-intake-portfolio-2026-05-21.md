**About Stripe:**
Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities.

**About the Team:**
People Strategy & Enablement bridges strategy and operational impact. The team designs scalable programs across five core areas: strategic planning, pillar management, project management, change management, and organizational planning cadences.

**The Role:**
This position owns the intake, triage, and portfolio management systems for the Partners, Operations & Insights (POI) organization. You'll serve as the primary contact for work requests from four core teams: People Solutions, People Data Analytics & Research, People Partners, and Employee Relations & HR Compliance.

Key responsibilities include managing the end-to-end intake process, facilitating prioritization conversations, building portfolio visibility dashboards, and designing an AI agent to automate scoping, duplicate detection, and prioritization guidance.

**What You'll Do:**
- Operate the Jira-based intake system and track requests through completion
- Partner with requestors on scope and expectations
- Facilitate intake cadences and roadmap reviews
- Surface systemic patterns and risks to leadership
- Apply strategic judgment using organizational priorities and frameworks
- Develop capacity reporting and dashboards
- Build and deploy an intelligent AI intake assistant
- Improve requestor-facing documentation and materials

**Minimum Requirements:**
- 7+ years in project management, operations, strategy, or consulting
- Demonstrated experience designing intake or portfolio processes
- Strong analytical and systems-thinking abilities
- Proficiency in Jira or similar tools
- Proven experience using AI tools for workflow automation
- Excellent communication skills
- Experience managing senior stakeholder relationships
- Comfort in ambiguous environments
- 2+ years working within HR or People operations

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- AI agent or automation workflow building experience
- Prompt engineering and RAG concepts familiarity
- Portfolio management background
- Change management framework experience
- HR systems familiarity (Workday, Greenhouse, Retool)
- Dashboard building capabilities (Tableau, Looker, Google Sheets)
- Advanced Google Workspace proficiency
- Employee data privacy and compliance awareness
