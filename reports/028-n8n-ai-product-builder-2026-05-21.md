**Title:** AI Product Builder
**Company:** n8n
**Location:** Remote-first, Berlin base; 25 European countries eligible including Netherlands
**Type:** Full-time

AI Trust: Build evals, observability, traces, guardrails, and human-in-the-loop patterns to transform AI from "works in demos" to production-ready.

- Harness literacy: understand tool loops, planners, scaffolding, context management
- Product instincts: clear thinking on trade-offs and user value
- UX taste: recognize good design and articulate why
- Ambiguity comfort: structure fuzzy problems effectively
- Clear communication: write well and reason from first principles

**Senior Candidate Expectations:**
- Production AI shipping experience with real users and failures learned from
- 0-to-1 product ownership through discovery, launch, iteration
- SQL, funnels, metrics fluency with healthy skepticism
- Cross-functional alignment without direct authority

**Early-Career Focus:**
- High learning and shipping velocity
- Non-traditional PM mindset flexibility
- Deep AI tinkering via solo or side projects
- Public demonstrations: demos, writing, talks, open-source

**Nice-to-Have Background:**
Workflow automation, open-source experience, founding experience, coding skills, growth experimentation, public visibility.

**Benefits Package:**
- Competitive pay with equity

- Remote with periodic offsites
- 2024 eNPS: 94
